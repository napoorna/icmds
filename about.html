About ICMDS

OUR STORY

ICMDS was established solely with a purpose of promoting Indian Classical Music and Dance, organize shows from local talent and renowned artists from India. ICMDS attempts to increase awareness of the Indian Classical arts in educational institutions in the Triangle area, concentrating on Wake county. As part of this program, we organize lecture demonstrations by visiting artists in schools.

01.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

We at ICMDS believe that it is important to maintain our heritage and let present and future generations learn, enjoy and appreciate Indian Classical Music and Dance. It is important to have a movement which promotes Indian Classical Music and Dance and save it from being diluted by Bollywood or Western influence.

02.

OUR PROMISE

We promise to our members and the community that we will remain dedicated towards our mission of promoting Indian Classical Music and Dance. We will also remain committed to bring a variety of shows for our members within our charter.

03.

PRIVACY POLICY

We are committed to protect the privacy of our members. We do not collect any personal information on this web site other than what is absolutely required. We do not share or sell our membership information with any third party. Some features of this web site may require to maintain cookies. Please feel free to contact any of the Board members if you have any questions on ICMDS privacy policy.
